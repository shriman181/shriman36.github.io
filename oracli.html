The Problem

Expert witnesses are the linchpin of litigation. Their prep is stuck in 1995.

In complex litigation, a single expert can make or break a matter worth hundreds of millions. Yet the preparation workflow — the most critical phase — remains almost entirely manual.

Experts arrive underprepared
A paralegal drops 300,000 documents into a shared folder. The expert reads what they can. The attorney calls for a two-hour prep session the night before deposition. This is the industry standard.
Prior testimony becomes a liability
Opposing counsel has read every word of the expert's prior testimony across every case. The expert has not. A single prior statement — three years and forty cases ago — dismantles the entire opinion under cross.
The one paragraph no one read
Out of 400,000 pages of discovery, opposing counsel finds the one document that contradicts the expert's central opinion. They were always going to find it. The question is whether your expert finds it first.

Intelligence Architecture

From raw documents to deposition-ready in hours.

Oracli's five-layer architecture transforms a case's full document universe into a living, contradiction-aware knowledge graph — then weaponizes it for expert preparation at every stage of litigation.

01
Universal Ingestion
Native PDFs, scanned depositions, medical records, financial models, engineering schematics — every document type processed and extracted at the entity and assertion level with domain-specific OCR pipelines tuned for legal, medical, financial, and engineering content.
02
Knowledge Graph Construction
Facts, claims, entities, and dates become nodes. Typed relationships — supports, contradicts, cites, qualifies — become edges. The entire case becomes a structured intelligence layer with a traversable citation graph, not a document collection.
03
Contradiction Detection
The Contradiction Engine continuously scans the knowledge graph — surfacing conflicts between the expert's current opinions and their prior testimony, published papers, and the full case record. Every finding ranked by severity and cited to the exact page and line.
04
Adversarial Simulation
A customized deposition simulation generated from detected vulnerabilities, the opposing attorney's known deposition style, the judge's Daubert preferences, and domain-specific impeachment patterns calibrated for medical, patent, financial, or environmental testimony.
05
Report Defensibility Review
Before the expert's written report is served, Oracli scores every opinion for evidentiary support, internal logical consistency, citation accuracy, scope risk, and language that opposing counsel will mine under cross. A Defensibility Score drives targeted remediation before service.

Founder

Built by someone who
understood the cost of failure.

"Litigation support has been transformed by technology at every layer — discovery, research, brief writing. Expert witness preparation remained untouched. Not because it's unimportant, but because it's hard. We built Oracli to do the hard thing."

Oracli was founded after observing a recurring pattern across complex litigation: expert witnesses with irreproachable credentials, dismantled under cross-examination not because their opinions were wrong — but because preparation left known vulnerabilities unaddressed. Prior testimony they hadn't reviewed. A paper they had authored that opposing counsel had read more carefully than they had. A document buried on page 187,000 of a 300,000-page production.

The legal industry's willingness to invest $2M in expert fees while spending two hours on preparation represented a structural failure that intelligence technology could close. Oracli was built to close it — not as a general-purpose legal AI, but as a purpose-built reasoning environment that understands exactly what an expert witness is, what their exposure is, and where they are most vulnerable before opposing counsel finds out first.
